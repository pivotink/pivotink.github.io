Things we've done

api.gov.au
A single place for all Government APIs

We were asked to modernise how software developers interact with Government. We came up with api.gov.au.

api.gov.au is the single place where Government defines it's APIs and the data they need.

Here is how we helped:

  • Setup and trained a small multidisciplinary team and guided them through the Digital Service Standard
  • Lead the user research, service design, protototyping through discovery and alpha
  • Implemented and operated the beta and live versions of the products
  • Managed the community of users and onboard new agencies

And everything in between.

We also built the definitions catalogue

It's the place where Government defines it's digital data. Here, anyone can find the data definitions used by Government on their APIs. This helps Government not only share data more effecitvely, but they can standardise on the information they collect from others, reducing duplicate reporting and double handling.

Pivot Lab
A new approach to API and data modelling

Manage your data models as code:

  • Everything you create in Pivot Lab is done in code. There are no manual or interactive congfiguration tools, only simple, declaritive text.
  • In Pivot Lab, versioning, importing, exporting and transformation is very simple, and very fast.

Everything you need to build, manage and publish APIs:

  • Search and reuse definitions from the Government Definitions Catalogue
  • Keep your data consistent by copy and pasting your previously used structures, following a schema-on-read approach
  • Realtime syntax preview of your models
  • Automatic and realitme generation of diagrams
  • Release generated models on github, after preflight-checking you're happy with them
